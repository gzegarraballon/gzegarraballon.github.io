Q: Why are you interested in Graphic Design?

A: I'm interested in Graphic Design because I like it's mixture of art and technology. I want to learn how to build websites, create apps, and design effective logos.

Q: What are 3 hobbies you enjoy?

A: I enjoy: photography/film, cooking, traveling.

Q: Who is your favorite artist? Why?

A: My favorite artist is Frida, because I love that she embodies strength and passion, and shows it through her work. She also ties me to my heritage. She was an incredibly gifted painter.

Q: If you won $1 million, what's the first thing you would buy? Why?

A: If I won a million dollars, I would buy a studio apartment in New York City, along with a ton of art supplies. Then I would have a private space to create freely.

Q: If you could live anywhere in the world, where would it be? Why?

A: If I could live anywhere in the world, I would want to live in the Oia Village in Santorini, Greece. I'm fascinated by Greek history and art, and it's beautiful there.
