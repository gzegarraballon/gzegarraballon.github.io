The White Shoes

Danielle Guizio is a young designer who is from New Jersey and has started her brand in New York City. She started with very simple graphic tees, duffle bags and phone cases with funny slogans. She then began to start making sweatsuits that got a lot of attention on social media when different celebrities got photographed wearing them. She continues to create high quality sweatsuits and different basics with simple graphics.
