The White Shoes

The Arrivals is a brand of mainly outerwear that is a sleek design made for the coldest weather. It started in NYC in 2014 and they create “foreverwear”by combining functionality with high quality craft to make coats that are meant to last a lifetime. Although pricey (their price point for a coat ranges from $450-$1200) their designs are simple and timeless. They have 3 stores, one in New York, one in Los Angeles, and one in San Fransisco, all beautifully designed.
