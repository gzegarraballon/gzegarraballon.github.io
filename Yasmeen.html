Q: What is your dream job and why?

A: My dream job is to able to apply different fields of design such as graphic design, fashion, and photography into my work and to communicate with clients on an international scale.

Q: Did you always know you wanted to major in a design related field or how did you discover you wanted to?

A: I have always loved design in all of its contexts, whether it be graphic design, fashion, or architecture. I chose Communications Design because I felt like I could explore a broader range of design aspects through this major.

Q: Which is your favorite place in the world and why?

A:My favorite place in the world is my hometown of Kuwait. I grew up there so it's my comfort zone.

Q: What's the thing that you love most about New York?

A: The thing I love most about New York is the endless opportunities that exist here. There is something here for everyone and I love that.

Q: Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

A: In 5 years, hopefully I will be getting started on building a name for myself and starting my own design studio in Kuwait.
